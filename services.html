Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while a comprehensive brand identity and website can take 6-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide through video calls, email, and project management tools. Remote collaboration has been a core part of my practice for years, and I've developed efficient processes to ensure smooth communication.

What's included in your pricing?

All packages include design, development, revisions (as specified), and initial setup. Additional services like ongoing maintenance, content updates, or advanced features are quoted separately. I'm transparent about all costs upfront.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! All packages include a support period (varies by package). After that, I offer ongoing maintenance and support services. Many clients choose to work with me long-term for updates, improvements, and new features.

Can you help with existing projects?

Absolutely! I often work on redesigns, improvements, and additions to existing websites and applications. I can audit your current setup and provide recommendations for improvements.

What technologies do you use?

I work with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React, Vue.js, Node.js, and various design tools like Figma and Adobe Creative Suite. I choose the best tools for each project's specific needs.
