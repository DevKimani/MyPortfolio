Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with me

How do you approach new projects?

I start with a discovery call to understand your goals, target audience, and requirements. Then I create a detailed project plan with timelines and milestones. Communication is key throughout the process.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Please share your project goals, target audience, timeline, budget range, and any specific requirements or inspiration. The more details you provide, the better I can tailor my proposal.

Do you work with clients outside the US?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide. All communication and project management is done remotely using video calls, email, and project management tools.

What's your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project scope. A simple website takes 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive projects can take 6-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! All projects include a support period, and I offer ongoing maintenance packages. Many clients choose to work with me long-term for updates and improvements.

Can you help with existing projects?

Definitely! I often work on redesigns, improvements, and additions to existing websites and applications. I can audit your current setup and provide recommendations.
